Home Energy Program

Questionnaire


This website acts as a convenient starting point to understanding what home energy programs you may be eligible for on both a federal and provincial level. After answering some basic profiling questions regarding your current living situation, you'll be shown all applicable energy programs that you may be eligible for.

This questionnaire is intended for those living in a single family home in the province of Nova Scotia. If you own a multi-residential home, or own rental units, and have questions regarding your eligibility, please contact Efficiency Nova Scotia for more information.

Please note: This questionnaire is not meant to be 100% accurate or override any decision from any provincial and/or federal clean energy program providers. Please speak with these providers to ensure you meet all the programs requirements before proceeding.
